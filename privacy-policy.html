Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective Date: December 16, 2024
Last Updated: December 16, 2024

1. Introduction

Easy Habit Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Easy Habit Tracker (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

Easy Habit Tracker is designed with complete privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Contact lists or social media information
  • Photos or media files
  • Any personally identifiable information

2.2 Usage Data

We do not collect any usage data, analytics, or tracking information. Easy Habit Tracker operates completely offline and does not send any data to external servers.

2.3 Habit Data

All your habit tracking data is stored exclusively on your device. This includes:

  • Habit names and descriptions you create
  • Completion dates and streak information
  • Progress statistics and analytics
  • App preferences and settings

Important: This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any data, we do not use your information in any way. Your habit data is processed entirely on your device to:

  • Provide app functionality: Your habit data is processed locally on your device to track and display your habits
  • Generate statistics: All progress analytics and statistics are calculated locally on your device
  • Store preferences: App settings and preferences are saved locally on your device

No data processing occurs on external servers.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage Only

All your habit data is stored exclusively on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. This means:

  • Your habit data never leaves your device
  • We have zero access to your personal habit information
  • Data is protected by iOS security features
  • You maintain complete control over your data
  • No cloud storage or external servers are used

4.2 No External Data Storage

Easy Habit Tracker does not use any external data storage services, including:

  • iCloud sync (not implemented)
  • Cloud databases or storage
  • Remote servers or analytics platforms
  • Third-party data storage services

4.3 Security Approach

Our security approach is simple and effective:

  • No data collection = No data breach risk
  • Data is secured by iOS's built-in encryption
  • No network transmission of personal data
  • App operates completely offline

5. Third-Party Services

Easy Habit Tracker does not use any third-party services. We do not use:

  • Analytics services (including Apple Analytics)
  • Crash reporting services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Any external APIs or services

Complete Privacy: Since we don't collect any data and don't use third-party services, your information is never shared with anyone.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Full Access: You can view all your habit data within the App at any time
  • Complete Control: You can delete individual habits or all data within the App
  • Data Ownership: All data belongs to you and stays on your device
  • No External Data: There is no external data to request or delete
  • Total Privacy: Uninstalling the App removes all data permanently

Note: Since we don't collect any data outside your device, there are no external data requests needed.

7. Children's Privacy

Easy Habit Tracker is completely safe for children of all ages because we do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since all data stays on the device, there are no privacy concerns related to data collection or transmission.

8. International Users

Easy Habit Tracker is available worldwide with complete privacy protection. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we don't collect any information, there are no international data transfer concerns. Your data never leaves your device regardless of your location.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing notice through the App (for significant changes)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
