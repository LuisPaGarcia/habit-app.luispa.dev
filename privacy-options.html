Disable Analytics Sharing:

  1. Go to iOS Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security
  2. Tap "Analytics & Improvements"
  3. Turn off "Share iPhone Analytics"
  4. Turn off "Share iCloud Analytics"

Control App-Specific Data:

  1. Go to iOS Settings ‚Üí Easy Habit Tracker
  2. Review and adjust any available privacy settings
  3. Disable background app refresh if desired
