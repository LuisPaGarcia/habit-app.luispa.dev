Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new habit?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen, enter your habit name, choose how often you want to do it, and tap "Save". Your new habit will appear in your list immediately.

Can I edit or delete habits?

Yes! Tap and hold any habit to see options to edit or delete it. You can change the name, frequency, or completely remove habits you no longer need.

How are streaks calculated?

Streaks count consecutive days you've completed a habit. If you miss a day, your streak resets to 0. The app also tracks your longest streak to celebrate your best performance.

Is my data backed up?

All your habit data is stored locally on your device and automatically backed up to iCloud if you have iCloud enabled. This means your data is safe and syncs across your Apple devices.

Can I export my data?

Currently, data export isn't available, but it's a frequently requested feature that we're considering for future updates. Your feedback helps us prioritize new features.
